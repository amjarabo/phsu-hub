PHSU Programs & Admissions Knowledge Base

Complete employee reference for all programs, requirements, tuition & key facts. Last synced from phsu.edu · April 2026

General Admissions Overview
Eligibility Requirements
  • High school diploma, bachelor's, or advanced degree (program-dependent)
  • Minimum GPA per program (see individual cards below)
  • Prerequisite coursework: biology, chemistry, mathematics for health programs
  • MCAT, GRE, or TOEFL scores as required by program
Application Checklist (All Programs)
  1. Official transcripts from all prior institutions
  2. Letters of recommendation (professors or professionals)
  3. Personal statement (goals, achievements, why PHSU)
  4. CV or résumé with academic, work & volunteer experience
  5. Standardized test scores (MCAT / GRE / TOEFL if applicable)
  6. Application fee
  7. Immunization records (required pre-enrollment)
Financial Aid Overview
  • Federal loans (FAFSA required)
  • Merit-based scholarships (program-specific)
  • Need-based grants
  • $8,000 scholarships available for MSMS Online & Remote
  • Dedicated Financial Aid Advisors available
  • Payment due on or before registration
Financial Aid Portal
Office of Admissions: For program-specific questions, prerequisites, and deadlines — contact admissions directly. Direct students to phsu.edu/apply.php for the online application portal. Catalog: catalog.phsu.edu
